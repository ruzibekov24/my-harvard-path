10 ta asosiy ibora

Kundalik suhbatda tez-tez kerak bo'ladigan qisqa jumlalar.

Iboralar 1–5
  • I'm running late — Men kechikayapman.
  • I'd appreciate it if… — Agar ... qilsangiz, minnatdor bo'lardim.
  • That's a good point — Bu yaxshi fikr.
  • I'm not entirely convinced — Men to'liq ishonganim yo'q.
  • It's up to you — Bu senga bog'liq.
Iboralar 6–10
  • I've changed my mind — Fikrimni o'zgartirdim.
  • I can't make it — Men yetib bora olmayman.
  • Let me get this straight — Keling, aniqlashtirib olamiz.
  • That's not necessarily true — Bu doim ham to'g'ri emas.
  • It's a win-win situation — Bu barcha uchun foydali holat.

Phrasal verbs

"GET" bilan yasaladigan iboralar

Speaking va Writing'da tabiiy eshitilish uchun eng foydali "get" iboralaridan biri.

  • Get out (of) — chiqmoq, tark etmoq
    He quickly got out of the car and ran inside.
  • Get along (with) — yaxshi chiqishmoq
    I get along well with my colleagues.
  • Get over — qiyinchilikni yengib o'tmoq
    It took him a while to get over the flu.
  • Get by — arang eplamoq
    Many people get by on a small salary.
  • Get across — fikrni yetkazmoq
    He found it hard to get his ideas across to the team.
  • Get rid of — qutulmoq
    I need to get rid of these old clothes.
Idiomalar
  • Once in a blue moon — juda kam hollarda
  • Bite the bullet — qiyin vaziyatga jasorat bilan chidamoq
  • The core of… — asosiy mazmuni / mohiyati (IELTS essayda foydali)
  • To show off — ko'z-ko'z qilish, maqtanib ko'rsatish

Passiv shakl · IELTS

It is widely believed that… — umumiy fikrlarni bildirish uchun rasmiy shakl. Masalan: It is generally accepted that exercise benefits mental health.

IELTS Writing Task 2

Tayyor "Totally Agree" shablon (220+ so'z)

Kirish: It is widely debated whether [savolni parafraz qiling]. I completely agree that [fikringiz] is the most sensible approach, primarily because [sabab 1] and [sabab 2].

Asosiy qism 1: eng kuchli argumentingizni misol bilan yoying (65–70 so'z).

Asosiy qism 2: ikkinchi argumentni qo'llab-quvvatlovchi misol bilan yoying (65–70 so'z).

Xulosa: In conclusion, although some might argue that [qarama-qarshi fikr], the advantages of [fikringiz] are far more compelling and practical.

Kirish40–45 so'z
Asosiy qism2 × 65–70 so'z
Xulosa50–55 so'z
Jami220+ so'z
Speaking

Speaking'da hayajonni yengish

Ko'pchilik Speaking'da past ball oladi, lekin sabab ko'pincha til bilmaslik emas — hayajon. Buni yengishning 4 ta amaliy usuli.

1 — Kundalik mashq

Har kuni 1 daqiqalik ovozli mashq qiling. Telefoningizga oddiy mavzuda ("your hobby", "your hometown") gapirib yozib oling. Maqsad — mukammal emas, to'xtamasdan davom ettirish.

2 — Fikrlashni o'zgartiring

"Bu suhbat, imtihon emas" deb fikrlang. Examiner sizdan mukammallik emas, tabiiy gapirishni kutadi — bu fikr hayajonni sezilarli kamaytiradi.

3 — Jim qolmang

To'xtab qolsangiz, "Let me think about that" yoki "That's an interesting question" kabi gaplar bilan vaqt oling — bu tabiiy, examiner buni salbiy baholamaydi.

4 — O'zingizni tinglang

Ovozingizni yozib, qayta tinglang. Bu hayajon va xatolarni tashqaridan ko'rish imkonini beradi — dastlab noqulay, lekin eng tez rivojlanish usuli shu.

Speaking · Describing pictures

Rasm tasvirlash uchun tayyor iboralar

Ikki rasmni taqqoslash vazifasida ball oshiradigan durdona birikmalar va 45 soniyalik universal shablon.

Durdona birikmalar
  • Catches my attention — diqqatimni tortdi
  • It seems to me that… — menga shunday tuyuladiki
  • Gives the impression of… — taassurot beradi
  • Judging by the expression on her/his face — yuzidagi ifodadan xulosa qilsak
  • In contrast to / Unlike the first photo… — birinchi suratdan farqli ravishda
  • Must be… — bo'lsa kerak
45 soniyalik shablon · 1/2
  • Kirish (5s): "There are two pictures, and I'm going to describe and compare them."
  • 1-rasm (12s): "In the first picture, I can see [odamlar soni] who are [faoliyat]. They look [his-tuyg'u], and the place seems to be [joy]."
45 soniyalik shablon · 2/2
  • 2-rasm (12s): "In the second picture, there are [odamlar soni], and they are [faoliyat]. The setting looks like [joy]."
  • O'xshashlik (8s): "Both pictures show people engaged in everyday activities."
  • Farq (8s): "The main difference is the setting and the number of people."
Speaking

Gap tuzish formulalari va why-why-how texnikasi

Speaking savoliga to'liq va tuzilgan javob berish uchun 5 ta tayyor formula, hamda javobni chuqurlashtirish texnikasi.

5 ta formula
  • Fikr: I think/believe/guess that + S + V
  • Sabab: This is mainly because + S + V
  • Misol: For example, + S + V
  • Taqqoslash: Compared to A, B is + sifatlovchi
  • Tajriba: I remember (V-ing) / I once (V2)
Why-why-how texnikasi

Savolga javob berganingizdan so'ng, 2 ta sabab ("why") ayting, keyin ular qanday amalga oshganini ("how") tushuntiring.

"I love spring because it's warm. Warm weather makes me feel happy. My birthday is also in spring. I usually go for walks in the park."

Formal xat xulosalari
  • Shikoyat: "I trust you will treat this matter with the urgency it deserves."
  • So'rov: "I would be grateful if you could consider my request."
  • Uzr: "Please accept my sincere apologies for the inconvenience caused."
  • Taklif: "I hope you find my suggestions helpful."

Reading strategiyasi

Reading Part 3: heading matching texnikasi

Paragraflarga sarlavha moslashtirish vazifasida vaqtni tejaydigan 5 qadamli usul.

1

Avval barcha sarlavhalarni o'qib, har birining asosiy g'oyasini aniqlang (masalan, "Environmental problems in cities" ≈ "Urban pollution issues").

2

Paragrafni to'liq emas, strategik o'qing — har birining birinchi va oxirgi gapiga e'tibor bering, asosiy g'oya odatda shu yerda bo'ladi.

3

Tafsilotga aldanmang — heading har doim asosiy fikrga mos keladi, alohida misol yoki detalga emas.

4

Sinonim va parafrazga tayyor bo'ling — test so'zlari matndagi so'zlar bilan aynan mos kelmaydi, ma'no bo'yicha qidiring.

5

Shubhali paragraflarni keyinga qoldiring, avval aniq mos keladiganlarni belgilang — shunda tanlov qisqaradi.
